Viktor's Bulk Image Compressor

Compress and convert images to JPEG, WebP, or PNG, in bulk with a chosen quality level, with EXIF metadata preserved. Great for photographers, so you can take high quality photos, but then back them up in lower quality if you don't need all that data!
