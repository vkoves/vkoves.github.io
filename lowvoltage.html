Low Voltage

Low Voltage is a radio show on Tuesdays from 9pm to 10pm on WIIT focusing on the softer side of Electronic music. Tune in for a wide variety of music from the softer sounds of well known artists like deadmua5 and Daft Punk to less recognized artists like Breakbot and Royksopp.
